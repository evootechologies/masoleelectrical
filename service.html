Our Services

General Electrical Maintenance

Our routine maintenance services help identify potential hazards before they become problems. From circuit breaker checks to outlet testing and light fixture servicing, we ensure your residential or commercial property stays safe, up to code, and energy efficient.

House Wiring

Whether you're building a new home or upgrading an old one, our team provides expert wiring services designed to handle modern electrical demands safely. We follow SANS standards to ensure each installation is safe, durable, and capable of supporting your lifestyle needs.

Fault Finding & Troubleshooting

Lights dimming? Outlets not working? Our trained electricians use advanced diagnostic tools to quickly locate faults and fix issues with minimal disruption. We find the root cause — not just the symptoms — to ensure lasting solutions.

Electric Fence Installation

Enhance your property’s perimeter with robust, high-voltage electric fencing installed to meet all safety and legal standards. Ideal for residential and commercial security, our systems are reliable, tamper-resistant, and visually discreet.

Switches & Socket Installations

Need more power points or stylish switch upgrades? We install modern, user-friendly, and safe switchgear that blends seamlessly with your interior. Perfect for home renovations, office fit-outs, or new builds.

Prepaid Meter Installation

Gain control of energy costs with our prepaid meter installation services. Perfect for landlords, rental units, and energy-conscious households, our meters provide transparent tracking and easy top-ups for electricity usage.

Day & Night Sensor Installation

Improve your property's energy efficiency and safety with automated day/night sensors. These systems automatically adjust lighting based on natural light conditions — ideal for outdoor lights, security areas, and garden pathways.
